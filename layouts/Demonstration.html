Layout Description:
Our purpose-built Demonstration Layout includes four OO gauge and three O gauge tracks and is always a hit at club nights and exhibitions. It allows for the easy testing of any stock or controllers and you see a larger verity of trains running. It also has a lift up section allowing for easy access in and out of the layout.
