NDMRC Exhibition 2026:


⬤ Saturday 18th July
⬤ Roade Village Hall
⬤ 10:00 - 16:00
⬤ Adult £6
⬤ Children Free
⬤ 11 Layouts
⬤ 6 Traders
⬤ 3 Society
⬤ Free Heritage Bus Ride
⬤ Raffle £1 per ticket
⬤ Catering Truck
⬤ Ice Cream Truck
You will see a variety of layouts on display depicting different scales, time periods and locations. The layouts are all different to last year and we know the operators have put in many hours of work to complete the layouts.
There is also a variety of traders attending, so we am sure there is something of interest to everyone. Whilst it is great that online sales are available, we don't think you can beat actually touching and seeing the articles before you buy. And of course you can ask their advice and guidance. They may just have that bargain that you have been looking for !!
Outside there is a vintage bus from Northampton Transport Heritage. They have kindly agreed to offer free rides around the village so feel free to hop on and grab a different view of the village.
